Our history

Penguin Air was founded by Gregory Smithson after returning to his hometown of Houston, Texas from an expedition to the South Pole. His goal was to create efficient, yet highly effective air conditioners that would make a even a penguin feel comfortable in the Texas heat.

The Penguin Promise

The Penguin Promise is our promise to all of our customers that we will take any action necessarry to ensure our customer is happy with their purchase. If we are unable to meet a customer's needs, for any reason, we will offer a full refund. Customer satisfaction is our top priority!

Ice Cold Science

All purchases from PenguinAir directly benefit scientific research at the South Pole! We understand the importance of scientific research and are commited to supporting scientists working in an environment as cold as our headquarters.
